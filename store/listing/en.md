## Full description (max 4000 chars)
Convert currencies the calm way. Niduna Currency Converter gives you fast,
reliable exchange rates for 40 world currencies and 11 cryptocurrencies —
with no account, no tracking by us, and a warm, editorial design that is
easy on the eyes.

**Convert instantly**
Type an amount once and see it converted to all your chosen currencies at
the same time. Add, remove, and reorder currencies freely. Swipe a row to
pin it to your favorites or make it the base currency.

**Charts that tell the story**
Up to 2 years of daily history for fiat pairs and 1 year for crypto.
Six time ranges, clean full-bleed charts, high/low/change at a glance.

**Your favorites, one tap away**
Pin the pairs you live in — home currency to travel currency, salary to
savings — and check them without thinking.

**Privacy first, really**
• No account, no sign-in, nothing to set up
• Your settings and favorites never leave your device
• No analytics or tracking by us
• Ads in the free version are non-personalized

**Works offline**
Rates and charts are cached on your device, so the app keeps working when
your connection doesn't.

**Thoughtful details**
• Light and dark mode, following your system if you like
• 5 languages: English, Deutsch, Español, Français, Italiano
• Reliable data: European Central Bank rates via Frankfurter, open
  crypto data — updated daily

Niduna Currency Converter is built for travelers, expats, and anyone who
moves between currencies — by an independent developer who believes a
utility app can be both useful and beautiful.
